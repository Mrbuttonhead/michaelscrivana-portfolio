e2e product design

"Twelve + You" is a supplement product for which I led the end-to-end design process during its pilot launch. Collaborating closely with the Art Director and Marketing team, I developed all visuals, including digital touchpoints like email campaigns, web content, and print materials such as brochures. To manage budget constraints, I utilized 3D rendering to design and visualize the packaging structure, ensuring a cohesive and impactful brand presence across digital and physical platforms.
