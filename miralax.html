MiraLAX — Brand Redesign

Led the visual refresh of the MiraLAX brand family — the #1 physician-recommended laxative — across packaging, ecommerce, retail displays, and digital surfaces. The redesign modernized a legacy brand while preserving shelf recognition, resulting in measurable sales growth.

Challenge

MiraLAX needed a brand refresh that could stand out in a crowded OTC aisle while maintaining the trust and recognition built over decades. The redesign had to scale across packaging formats, ecommerce listings, in-store displays, and digital campaigns — all within a 3-week sprint.

Role

Lead designer — responsible for the packaging redesign, brand world visuals, ecommerce asset creation, and retail display design. Coordinated with brand managers and external vendors to ensure consistency across all touchpoints.

Outcome

$48M in retail sales · $67M projected · Accounted for over half of MiraLAX brand growth · Completed in a 3-week design sprint
